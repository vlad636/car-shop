HIGH QUALITY CAR SALES – THE HOME OF QUALITY USED CARS IN BUCHAREST

High Quality Car Sales is a family owned and operated Used Car Dealership located in the center of Bucharest.

We are a passionate and friendly team who pride ourselves on being able to offer outstanding customer service and accommodate to ensure all our customers have a positive car buying experience. We stock a variety of used High Quality vehicles to fit any lifestyle, need or budget.

HQCS is one of only a few small family owned used car dealerships in Romania who offer RAA inspected vehicles, giving you peace of mind when purchasing your next vehicle with us. We are a proud member of the Motor Trade Association, Romania which enables us to be up to date and complaint when it comes to our staff, customers and industry standards.

We offer Finance solutions to help you drive home sooner or if you want to Sell Your Car, trade-ins are welcome!

If you have any questions about any of our used cars for sale or services, please Contact a member of our helpful team today. Alternatively, feel free to drop by our used car dealership in Piata Victoriei Square during opening hours.
